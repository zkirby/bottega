Bottega is a maker house that is unlike anything else in the Bay Area. We are dedicated to bringing together students from across disciplines - from engineering to design - and building fun and interesting side projects for the sake of just making cool things. At the current moment, we’re partnering with Bed|n|Build in order to create a live-in makerspace so that anyone from all parts of campus has the opportunity to learn new skills, listen to cool speakers, and work on new undertakings. While we secure a house, we’re still going to do all we can to foster a culture of making on campus! Look out for some of our events next semester, including speaker panels, build nights, and more!
